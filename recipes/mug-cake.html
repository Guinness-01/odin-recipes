Mug cake

What's a good meal without a dessert? this is one of my favorites for a quick dessert when your craving something. Credit to all recipes

¼ cup all-purpose flour ¼ cup white sugar 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder ⅛ teaspoon baking soda ⅛ teaspoon salt 3 tablespoons milk 2 tablespoons canola oil 1 tablespoon water ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt together in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil, water, and vanilla extract.
  2. Cook in the microwave until cake is done in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
